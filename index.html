About Us

Welcome to the Association of STEM Educators in Nigeria (ASEN), a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young minds and nurturing future leaders in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics â€“ collectively known as STEM. At ASEN, we believe that every child, regardless of their background, should have the opportunity to explore and excel in these critical disciplines.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to bridge the gap between formal education and real-world skills demanded in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape. We are committed to providing hands-on learning experiences that ignite curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking abilities in our students. By empowering them with knowledge and the latest technologies, we aim to foster confidence and equip them with the skills necessary to thrive in the global STEAM arena.

OUR VISION

We envision a future where access to quality STEAM education is democratized, and barriers that limit potential are torn down. Through our initiatives, we aspire to make Nigeria a shining beacon of tech and innovation in Africa, where our students become not just beneficiaries of knowledge but also the drivers of transformational change.
